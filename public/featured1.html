Sun Soaked Home by the Sea

Positioned proudly at the top of the section, this sun drenched 230m2 home captures light, space, and effortless coastal living.

Built in 2008 with solid brick and tile construction and fully double glazed, this is a home designed for comfort and longevity. With two solar panels supporting hot water heating and underfloor heating adding warmth underfoot, year round comfort is assured.

Generous proportions throughout provide flexibility for growing families or those who simply appreciate room to move.

A large separate lounge enjoys sunny outlooks and opens seamlessly onto the easy care patio complete with artificial lawn, creating the perfect setting for relaxed afternoons or entertaining guests. The separate dining room offers versatility and could easily function as a second living space.

The kitchen is well appointed with exceptional storage and a charming sunny nook that invites morning coffee, casual dining, or a quiet reading corner.

The master suite is spacious and inviting, complete with ensuite and ample wardrobe space. Two additional bedrooms are generous in size and thoughtfully designed with excellent storage. A tiled and spacious main bathroom services the home, while the separate laundry provides practical access straight out to the washing line.

Internal double garaging adds further storage and convenience, rounding out a home that balances comfort with functionality.

All of this is set just a short stroll to Ōtaki Beach, with an easy drive back into Ōtaki township and quick access onto the expressway for commuting.

A solid, sun filled coastal home offering space, strength, efficiency, and superb location.

To be sold by Tender 18th of March 2026 at 1pm.
